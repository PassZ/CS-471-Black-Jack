Welcome to the Blackjack Tutorial!

Blackjack is a popular card game played in casinos. The goal is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

To start, you'll be dealt two cards. You can choose to "hit" to be dealt another card or "stand" to keep your current hand.

After you stand, the dealer will reveal their hand and draw additional cards until their hand totals 17 or higher.

If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's without going over, you win!

Remember:

  • Ace can count as 1 or 11 points, depending on your choice.
  • Cards 2 through 10 are worth their face value.
  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are each worth 10 points.

Enjoy the game and good luck!
